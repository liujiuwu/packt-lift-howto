Ajax Examples

Lift provides awesome, terribly easy to use Ajax- once you understand the way Lift deals with it.

Here's a minimal example:

<button id="ajax_example1" data-lift="AjaxExamples.example1">Click Me</button>

results in:

Click Me

 

Let's look at a slightly more complex example.
Let's build a simple Ajax form to enter a value and then let's add this value to a list here on the page: 


<form data-lift="form.ajax">
  <div data-lift="AjaxExamples.example2">
    <p>
      <label for="name">Your Name:</label><br>
      <input id="name" name="name">
    </p><p>
      <label for="city">Your City:</label><br>
      <input id="city" name="city">
    </p>
    <button type="submit">Submit</button>
  </div>
</form>
